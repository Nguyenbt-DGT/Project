Squad review draft Moto Companion App Prepared 14 Jul 2026

Moto Companion App (working title)

A mobile app for motorcycle touring riders and maintenance-minded bike owners. It brings vehicle health, trip planning, and route history together in one place — know when your bike needs service, plan your next tour around it, and record where you rode.

The loop

Three functions, one feedback loop

This is the mechanic that makes Moto Companion App one product, not three separate apps — HEALTH_CHECK sits at the center of both paths below.

Flow A — Riding updates vehicle health
MAP_TRACKING Ride recorded in real time
→
HEALTH_CHECK Distance added to vehicle total
→
HEALTH_CHECK Maintenance metrics re-checked
→
NOTIFY Reminder sent if threshold reached
Flow B — Planning a trip checks vehicle health
TOURING_PLAN New trip plan started
→
HEALTH_CHECK Selected vehicle's status checked
→
WARN User warned before departure if overdue

MAP_TRACKING feeds HEALTH_CHECK.   HEALTH_CHECK gates TOURING_PLAN.

The three functions

What each part of the app does

Equal parts of one product — shown here in the same order as the project brief.

HEALTH_CHECK

Know when your bike needs service

Log key technical metrics for your vehicle — distance traveled, coolant, engine oil, and other configurable items — set a maintenance interval and warning threshold per metric, and get notified when something is coming due or overdue.

  • Tracks distance, coolant, engine oil (+ more, extensible)
  • Per-metric interval: by km and/or by time
  • Per-metric warning threshold, user-configurable
  • Two severity levels: coming due soon / overdue
Open questions
Auto vs. manual distance tracking — pending Multi-vehicle support — pending Notification channel (in-app/email/SMS) — pending Extended metrics list (tires, brakes, chain, etc.) — pending confirmation
TOURING_PLAN

Plan tours that respect your bike's limits

Plan a touring trip — potentially multi-day, with stops and checkpoints along the route — and before departure, the app checks the selected vehicle's maintenance status and warns you if it's overdue for service.

  • Multi-day trips with stops / checkpoints
  • Pre-departure check against selected vehicle
  • Warns the rider if overdue, before leaving
Open questions
Auto route suggestion vs. manual pins — pending Shareable group trip plans — pending
MAP_TRACKING

Record every ride, replay it later

While you ride, the app records the trip in real time and redraws the path traveled on a map so you can review it afterward. Trip distance feeds back into the vehicle's total distance for HEALTH_CHECK.

  • Real-time trip recording while riding
  • Redraws the traveled path on a map
  • Trip distance feeds HEALTH_CHECK's total
